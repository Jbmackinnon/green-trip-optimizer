# Green Trip Optimizer

A mobile + web app (built in Flutter) for saving “future stops”, planning greener trips by combining saved stops, and tracking your CO₂ savings offline.

## Features
- Save destinations you want to visit later  
- Plan a trip between two locations and get suggested stops to add  
- Estimate your CO₂ savings from combining trips  
- Works offline and as a PWA (installable web app)  
- Minimal data & processing overhead — designed to stay green
